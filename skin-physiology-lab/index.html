Research

Skin barrier regeneration and inflammatory signaling

Exploring the mechanisms of skin barrier repair and inflammation using human skin cells.

Atopic dermatitis models using keratinocytes and fibroblasts

Developing in vitro models to study atopic dermatitis and test therapeutic strategies.

Effects of natural compounds (e.g., Reversine, SH-340)

Investigating the bioactivity of natural compounds on skin cell function and regeneration.

Microfluidic-based 3D skin models

Utilizing microfluidic technology to create advanced 3D skin models for research and drug testing.
